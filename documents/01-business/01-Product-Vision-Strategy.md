# Product Vision & Strategy

## Vision Statement

**"Empower businesses to transform inspiration into action by providing actionable insights into their user journey funnels."**

IAFA helps businesses understand exactly where users drop off in their conversion funnels, identify optimization opportunities, and make data-driven decisions to improve conversion rates.

## Mission

To provide a simple, powerful, and affordable analytics platform that makes funnel analysis accessible to businesses of all sizes - from startups to enterprises.

## Success Metrics (Business)

### Acquisition Metrics
- User signups per month
- Signup-to-activation rate (% who create first funnel)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Traffic sources and conversion rates

### Engagement Metrics
- Daily/Weekly/Monthly Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU)
- Average funnels per user
- Average events tracked per user
- Dashboard views per user

### Retention Metrics
- User retention rate (Day 7, Day 30, Day 90)
- Churn rate
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Time to value (TTV) - how fast users get insights

### Revenue Metrics
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

### Product Metrics
- Feature adoption rate
- Funnel accuracy (tracking quality)
- Query performance (dashboard load times)
- System uptime (target: 99.5%+)

## Key Differentiators

1. **Parquet-Based MVP**: Faster development, lower costs, easier deployment
2. **Privacy-First**: No data sharing with third parties
3. **Customizable**: Define any funnel, track any event
4. **Developer-Friendly**: Simple API, clear documentation
5. **Transparent Pricing**: No hidden fees, pay for what you use

## Risks & Mitigation

### Market Risks
- **Competition**: Large players (Google Analytics, Mixpanel) have market share
  - *Mitigation*: Focus on simplicity, privacy, and affordability
- **Market Size**: Niche market may be small
  - *Mitigation*: Start niche, expand to adjacent markets

### Product Risks
- **Feature Gaps**: MVP may lack features users expect
  - *Mitigation*: Prioritize core features, gather feedback, iterate quickly
- **Technical Limitations**: File-based storage may not scale
  - *Mitigation*: Plan database migration path, monitor performance

### Business Risks
- **Customer Acquisition**: High CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
  - *Mitigation*: Focus on organic channels, referrals, content marketing
- **Churn**: Users may churn if value not clear
  - *Mitigation*: Onboarding flow, clear value proposition, quick time-to-value
